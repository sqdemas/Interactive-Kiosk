Personalized Algorithms

From social media apps to the self-checkout station at the grocery store, algorithms are integrated nearly everywhere. Users receive helpful recommendations to mitigate the issue of choice overload while companies employing algorithms can increase watchtime, promote sales, and utilize targeted advertising.
The ubiquity of these algorithms has propelled the recommendation engine industry into an incredibly expansive and influential market.
