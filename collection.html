Data Collection

All sorts of data is collected about users to create a profile of their habits both online and offline. Data is collected from three major sources:

  • Public sources: public social media profiles, online forums
  • Commercial sources: retailers, subscription lists, finanncial services, loyalty programs
  • Government sources: census, public records, and social security information

Data can be classified into explicit data and implicit data. Traditionally algorithms relied on explicit data— data provided by users like feedback or demographic information. However, many algorithms today rely on implicit data— data regarding user behavior as implicit data is always objective.
Examples of implicit data include:

  • Watch time of videos and scroll speed on social media
  • Search history and activity across websites monitored by tracking cookies
  • Shopping habits recorded by retail loyalty programs
